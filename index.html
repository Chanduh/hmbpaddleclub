About

Half Moon Bay Paddle Club is a members-only club nestled in the scenic coastal town of Half Moon Bay, California. Our club is dedicated to providing an exceptional paddling experience for water enthusiasts of all levels.

With our pristine natural surroundings, breathtaking ocean views, and a tight-knit community of paddlers, we offer the perfect haven for those seeking adventure, relaxation, and a sense of belonging.

Whether you are a seasoned paddleboarder or new to the sport, our club offers a variety of exciting events and activities to suit your interests.
